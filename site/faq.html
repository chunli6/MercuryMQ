F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions about MercuryMQ

Q: What towns do you deliver to?

A: We deliver to most towns in north Bergen county including: Ridgewood, Glen Rock, Ho ho kus, Paramus, Washing Twp., Westwood, Hillsdale, Woodcliff Lake, Park Ridge, River Vale, Old Tappan, Montvale, Upper Saddle River, Saddle River, Allendale, Mahwah, Oakland, Franklin Lakes, and Alpine. If you are outside the north Bergen area don’t worry - we are quickly expanding and hope to soon reach Connecticut.

Q: How far away can I order from?

A: You can order from anywhere, so long as we are able to get there and back in about 40 minutes. For example, an order to Ridgewood from Ramsey is fine, but an order to Mahwah would take too long and delay other orders.

Q: How soon will my order arrive?

A: Most orders arrive in approximately 40 minutes, while our best time so far is 20 minutes. We guarantee that your delivery will arrive in under an hour, or there is no delivery fee!

Q: I texted the number but didn’t get a response. Why?

A: If you haven’t received an automatic response text in 5 minutes and you have good cell signal, please resend your message.

Q: How much is the delivery fee?

A: Deliveries costs $6.00 when the cost of food is under $40.00. Every $10.00 increase in the cost of food increases the delivery fee by $2.00. For example, a $70.00 order would cost $12.00 in delivery fees.

Q: Do I need to tip the driver?

A: Our drivers certainly would not complain if you tipped them, but tips are not required or expected.

Q: I need to cancel/change my order. What should I do?

A: If you need to change or cancel your order, just text our number explaining the alteration, and ignore the “Improperly Formatted Order” automatic response. Someone from our team will contact you. We will do our best to help with any cancellations, but please keep in mind that we often call in the order right away, and your food may already be prepared. Customers that repeatedly cancel orders will be blocked by our system.

Q: I received a message saying my order was formatted incorrectly. Why?

A: Our ordering platform logs all delivery requests, and so the text messages must be in a specific format for the computer to understand and break down. Go to the “How it Works” tab for details on how to place an order.
