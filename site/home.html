What We Offer

The Delivery Board

Have you ever wanted food delivered from a restaurant that doesn't deliver? MercuryMQ will deliver food directly to your door from select local restaurants. Simply text your order, your address, and the restaurant that you would like to order from to 201-383-4611 and we will deliver your food. MercuryMQ Delivery costs only $6 per order, and your first order is free!

How to Order

Send a text message to 201-383-4611 with the following format:

  1. Restaurant Name
  2. Your Address
  3. Your Order

Be sure to include the numbers on the left when placing your order!
