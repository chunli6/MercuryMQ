What Are Tasks?

Tasks are an easy way to take care of errands from your phone. By simply texting a task to Mercury, you can have items delivered to your location conveniently once a week. Need light bulbs, batteries, and paper towels? Send a task to Mercury. Running low on dishwasher soap or shampoo? Submit a task to Mercury.

There is no need to sign up or register - just starting texting Mercury with tasks right away and we will deliver your items each Saturday!


How It Works

To submit a task, text 201-345-4820 with the following format:

1. Your location
2. The item(s) you want delivered

Each Saturday, between 4pm and 8pm, we will deliver the items to your location specified in the text message.


Pricing

Pricing is based on the total value of the items delivered on each drop off. The fee structure is as follows:

• $5 for items whose total cost is up to $30
• $2 additional for each $10 increment above $30

For example, if your items costs $25, the fee would be $5. If your items costs $25, the fee would be $7 and so on.
