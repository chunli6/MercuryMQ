GET FOOD FROM ANYWHERE DELIVERED TO YOUR DOOR

Have you ever wanted food delivered from a restaurant that doesn't deliver? MercuryMQ will deliver food directly to your door from select local restaurants. Simply text your order, your address, and the restaurant that you would like to order from to 201-383-4611 and we will deliver your food. MercuryMQ Delivery costs only $6 per order, and your first order is free!

To order, send a text message to 201-383-4611 with the following format (as shown):
1. Restaurant Name
2. Your Address
3. Your Order

Our founding:

On a beautiful summer afternoon, Dan was melting into the couch playing Call of Duty. It dawned on him to order food, but Dan didn't want to order pizza for the billionth time. In that moment, Mercury MQ was born. After many late nights of programming, Dan built a delivery service that would let anyone easily order food via text message. Thus, Chipotle burritos were delivered all across the land.

The Best Delivery Service:

Mercury MQ offers the fastest, simplest delivery service out there. No need to download any apps, or hope that your favorite restaurant is listed on a website grub service. We deliver from virtually anywhere. Your courier will receive a text with your order, and will drive to the restaurant immediately to collect the food. You can even order groceries and toiletries from your local supermarket and CVS. Just send a text message with what you want and where you want it from, and we’ll dispatch one of our drivers to bring it to your door!
